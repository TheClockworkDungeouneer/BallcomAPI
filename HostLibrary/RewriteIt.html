The Re-Write It Project
Join Us Today!Our Mission

A sustainable way to support Chicago's youth

We are a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization making essential learning materials more accessible for students across Chicago communities. By equipping youth with the materials they need in order to reach their fullest potential, we hope that each child can explore their avenues of self-expression, creativity, and intellect.

Volunteer

Become a volunteer to help distribute our donated school supplies to underserved youth institutions across the city.

Our organization wouldn't exist without the help of our wonderful volunteer team.
